Testimonials

  “I have been training with Julio since July 2009. I am 47 and had given up cigarettes and put on a bit of weight and decided that I wanted to get fit. I had not been to a gym in years but walked everywhere. My weight had been constant for 30 years so my aim really was just to get back to my normal weight and have more stamina.

Julio was also my son's tennis coach. I knew I could work with him because he doesn't take a boot camp approach. He listens to your own goals and helps get you there. I very quickly lost the few lbs weight bur started to get into the gym and working out. My next goal was to fit into a pair of jeans that hadn't fitted me n years and that was achieved quite easily. I started running a bit and decide to have a 5km run as my goal. I am running 10km on Saturday and with Julio's help plan to train for the half marathon. I never in a million years thought I would not only be doing this but loving it. I am far too bossy to have someone shout at me to make me work which is what I had thought personal trainers would be. It is really good to have a training plan and a goal as it encourages me to go to the gym a lot on my own. Having a personal trainer makes all the difference as the raining is tailored your age, weight, level of fitness and goals. Over the last 18 months I have worked on muscular strength, cardiovascular endurance, and flexibility, power, skill, and speed. I have exercised muscles I didn't know existed. Julio knows exactly when to push me hard and when I'm stressed what works outs make me feel better, boxing is a good one!

My son as developed a life long love of tennis because of Julio and because of my enthusiasm my husband has started to come to the gym with me so the whole family has benefited from my training. I have bags more energy, have dropped a dress size, changed body shape and feel great.

I cannot recommend Julio highly enough.”

Anna Casey



  “My husband and i had both worked hard to get into shape and lose weight. We decided we needed to step up a level with our exercise to help keep us motivated. So we decided to try a personal training session, initially we weren't very keen due to our pre-conceived ideas about personal trainers (macho, vain, bossy...sorry!), but after our first individual sessions with Julio we were hooked!

Julio has a unique style of personal training which caters to your individual needs, he is keen to understand your goals and works with you to achieve them. Julio has transformed both my husbands and my body shape and fitness levels.

Main attributes Julio brings to our fitness sessions include:

  • Well planned sessions, no two sessions are the same.
  • lots of variety in the exercises and equipment used.
  • A different approach to working out.
  • Julio pays attention to your technique and coaches you through the session to avoid you feeling uncomfortable and reducing injuries.
  • We are always working towards achieving our specific goals.
  • Julio has a real interest in your progress and success.
  • Julio monitors progress via fitness tests, before after photos/measurements - all very motivating!
  • Reviews your diet and provides advice as appropriate.

Julio is very good at building your confidence in the sessions so you are achieving more than you think your body and mind are capable of. We definitely recommend having Julio as a fitness trainer.”

Parm & Bobby Rai
