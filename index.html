Time and time again I come across people who meet me and the first thing they say when they find out I am a dentist is...."I hate going to the dentist!" then add as an after thought, "it's nothing personal!"

Dentistry has been around since atleast 7000 BC and yet in the 21st Centuary people still hate going.

I have been a practicing dentist for over 10 years. My passion is to introduce you to the concept of COMFORT dentistry, a world where having dental treatment is something to look forward to, I dare say, even to escape to! The age of hating or being anxious is gone as we enter this era of Soothing, Relaxing, Comfort dentistry.
Dr Busola Olubajo
